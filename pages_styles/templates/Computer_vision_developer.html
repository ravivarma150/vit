About the Course

Computer Vision is a field of artificial intelligence that trains computers to interpret and understand the visual world. This course is designed for those who want to learn how to develop applications that can process, analyze, and understand images and videos.

Course Highlights

  • Introduction to Computer Vision and its applications
  • Image Processing fundamentals
  • Feature detection and matching
  • Object detection and recognition using CNNs
  • Deep learning for Computer Vision
  • Video analysis and tracking
  • Introduction to OpenCV and other CV libraries
  • Projects and hands-on experience

Enroll in this course to gain practical experience and enhance your skills in Computer Vision.
